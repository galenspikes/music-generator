and the *why* of design choices. Read these to understand, not to do a task.*

  `prob`/`flam`, and the two-tier randomness model.
- **[The token system](token-system-report.md)** — what the notation is, its
  distinctive design decisions, and its provenance.
- **[Design decisions (ADRs)](decisions/)** — one record per major choice (the *why*).
- **[Song studies](song-studies/)** — literate walkthroughs of real pieces
