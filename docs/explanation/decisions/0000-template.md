# ADR-0000: <short title of the decision>

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by ADR-XXXX
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What problem or force prompted this decision? What constraints were in play? Keep
it factual — the situation as it was, not the conclusion.

## Decision

The choice that was made, stated plainly. One or two sentences.

## Rationale

*Why* this choice over the alternatives. List the options that were considered and
why they were rejected. This is the most valuable section — it's the reasoning a
future reader (or a reviewer) needs.

## Consequences

What this decision makes easy, and what it makes hard or forecloses. Include the
honest costs, not just the benefits.

## Status / follow-ups

Anything still open, or later ADRs that revisit this.
