*Round 2. Round 1 established: presets are the primary UX, controls are flat/all-visible,
the home is whatever preset you're starting from. These questions go deeper on presets,

## 1. Presets

**1a.** When you save a preset, what do you want to call it? (A name you type? An auto-name
from the chord + settings? A timestamp? Something else?)

**1b.** How do you find a preset later? (A list? A grid? Tags/categories? Search?)

**1c.** Is a preset the entire state of the instrument — every control — or just some of it?
(E.g. does BPM get saved, or is BPM always set per-session?)

**1d.** Can a preset be incomplete — e.g. "just the percussion settings, apply on top of
whatever is currently playing"? Or is a preset always a full snapshot?

**1e.** What's the relationship between a preset and a *song*? Is a song a sequence of
presets? Or are those completely different things?
